Born on May 10, 1969, in Mukah

About My Dad

  • Born and raised in Mukah
  • Despite not coming from a wealthy family, he never gave up on pursuing his education
  • Missed school in his sixth year but remarkably managed to qualify for secondary school
  • Decided to enter Form 6, after which he applied to IPG Kampus Batu Lintang to study Bahasa Melayu
  • Graduated at the age of 24
  • Almost 30 years of teaching experience
  • Stands as a paragon of excellence and has earned the revered title of "Otai" at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Dalat
